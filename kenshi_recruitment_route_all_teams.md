# Kenshi Recruitment Route — Team 1 Priority, Bo-Compatible, with Teams 2 and 3

## Scope and assumptions

- Vanilla Kenshi, unique recruits only, plus your custom main character.
- Primary objective: build **Team 1** as the recruiting/adventuring squad, with **Bo included**.
- Secondary objective: collect **Team 2** and **Team 3** efficiently along the travel route, dropping them at your main HQ when convenient.
- I assume an early-game start around **The Hub / Squin / Border Zone**, because that is the most common early progression route. If your HQ is elsewhere, keep the same order but use your HQ as the drop-off waypoint.
- I have avoided inventing unsupported quests. Where recruitment depends on a condition, I have listed the condition explicitly.
- Important correction from the previous plan: **Yamdu is not included**, because he requires turning in **Tinfist**, which conflicts with a pro–Anti-Slaver/Bo route.

## Critical Bo strategy

Bo is the key constraint. The safest strategic rule is:

> **Do as much neutral recruitment, shopping, blueprint buying, base preparation and UC-area recruitment as possible before officially joining the Anti-Slavers.**

### Why

- Bo is found in **Black Scratch**, in the **Tech Hunter Bar** or **Waystation**, accompanied by an Anti-Slaver Jonin.
- Bo can be recruited if the player is allied with the **Anti-Slavers** and has killed or imprisoned one of the listed anti-slavery targets: **Eyegore, Lady Kana, Longen, Emperor Tengu, one of the Slave Masters, or one of the nobles**.
- The Anti-Slavers can be joined by talking to **Grey** at **Spring**. This gives +75 Anti-Slavers relations but applies -30 relations with **United Cities**, **Slave Traders** and **Traders Guild**.
- Joining the Anti-Slavers too early can make UC-area recruiting, shopping and travel more awkward.
- Do **not** turn in or harm **Tinfist**, **Grey**, **Jaegar** or **Bo** if you want a clean Anti-Slaver route.
- Do **not** ally with United Cities, Traders Guild, Slave Traders or the Holy Nation before the Bo step. These alignments can lock or complicate Anti-Slaver dialogue paths.

### Recommended Bo route

1. Recruit and train Team 1 until it can travel safely, escape bad fights, carry prisoners, and survive UC/Slave Trader hostility.
2. Complete the UC-area recruit sweep first: Jewel, Izumi, Riddly, Red if desired, Headshot, Ray, and any other UC-area recruits you want.
3. Buy essential blueprints and supplies before burning relations with UC/Traders Guild.
4. Visit **Spring** and speak to **Grey** to ally with the Anti-Slavers.
5. Capture or kill one valid target for Bo’s requirement. The most route-efficient targets are usually a **Slave Master** or **noble** in the UC/Traders Guild/slaver ecosystem, but each is guarded.
6. Go to **Black Scratch** and recruit Bo.
7. After Bo joins, treat UC/Traders Guild territory as hostile or at least politically risky.

# Practical route summary

## Early route

1. Train main character around The Hub/Squin.
2. Recruit Ruka, Kang, Rane and Oron from Shek territory.
3. Optionally recruit Griffin before you travel with skeletons/hivers, if your party can safely enter Holy Nation towns.
4. Go north for Burn, Flotsam recruits, Fishing Village production recruits, and opportunistic Hobbs/Bard.
5. Go to Mongrel only once your squad can outrun or survive Fogmen; recruit Beep, Infinite Wingwang and Crumblejon.

## Mid route

6. Sweep the Swamp for Miu, Doctor Chung, Green, Hamut and Stubs Momuso.
7. Recruit Chad at Flats Lagoon.
8. Scout Black Scratch and buy blueprints, but do not rely on Bo until Anti-Slaver conditions are met.
9. Complete UC-area recruitment before joining Anti-Slavers: Jewel, Izumi, Headshot, Ray, and reserves if wanted.

## Late route

10. Break into Tengu’s Vault when your squad is strong/stealthy enough; rescue Luquin and Sinklyde together.
11. Join Anti-Slavers at Spring by talking to Grey.
12. Capture or kill a valid noble/slave target.
13. Recruit Bo at Black Scratch.
14. Complete Seto later via Shek Kingdom progression.

## Bo-specific warning checklist

Before joining Anti-Slavers:

- [ ] Recruit UC-area characters you still want.
- [ ] Buy important UC/Traders Guild/Black Scratch supplies and blueprints.
- [ ] Confirm you do not want Yamdu in this run.
- [ ] Do not ally with UC, Traders Guild, Slave Traders or Holy Nation.
- [ ] Do not harm Tinfist, Grey, Jaegar or Bo.
- [ ] Build enough stealth/lockpicking/athletics/combat to survive a noble/slave-master operation.

Before trying to recruit Bo:

- [ ] You are allied with Anti-Slavers.
- [ ] A valid noble/slave target has been killed or imprisoned.
- [ ] Bo is alive and present at Black Scratch.
- [ ] You are ready for UC/Traders Guild/Slave Trader consequences.
