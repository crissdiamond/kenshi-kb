# Kenshi recruitable NPC reference by role

**Scope:** vanilla Kenshi **named unique recruits** only. Generic recruits are not included because their names, stats and spawn pools are randomised.  
**Ordering principle:** best recruits are at the top of each role list, based on starting stats, usefulness for the role, recruitment difficulty and fit with the three-team plan.  
**Important caveat:** the wiki states that listed locations are possible spawn points, and some unique recruits may not spawn in a given game. Stats with `+/-` or “randomise” can vary.

**Main sources used:**
- Kenshi Wiki — Unique Recruits: https://kenshi.fandom.com/wiki/Unique_Recruits
- Kenshi Wiki — Bo: https://kenshi.fandom.com/wiki/Bo
- Kenshi Wiki — Anti-Slavers: https://kenshi.fandom.com/wiki/Anti-Slavers

### Suggested Team 2 crafting specialisations

Because the unique-recruit pool does not give you ready-made master smiths, assign long-term specialisations like this:

| Craft / resource capability | Recommended lead | Backup / trainee | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Weapon Smithing | **Green Finger** | Digna / Reva / Shryke | Green Finger has labour/farming/engineering foundations; smithing itself must be trained. |
| Armour Smithing | **Digna** | Reva / Hobbs | No unique recruit starts as a high armour smith; use stable HQ worker and train continuously. |
| Crossbow Smithing | **Shryke** | Red / Ells | No strong unique crossbow smith; choose a non-frontline recruit and keep them crafting. |
| Robotics crafting / repair logistics | **Izumi** | Doctor Chung / Cat | Science/medic profiles make them natural technical support. |
| Farming | **Miu** | Green Finger | Highest farming profile plus Blood Spider warning. |
| Cooking | **Jewel** | Miu / Green Finger | Jewel has the strongest cooking stat. |
| Engineering / construction | **Soman** | Green Finger | Soman has the strongest engineering stat. |
| Research | **Izumi** | Cat / Doctor Chung | Izumi has the strongest science among easy specialist recruits. |
| Medical | **Doctor Chung** | Cat / Izumi | All three have 40 Medic; split between HQ and field support as needed. |
| Hauling / logistics | **Pia** | Red / Ells / Hobbs | Pia’s Athletics and Strength are good for running and supply loops. |

## Recommended overall use of the list

1. **Start from The Hub / Squin:** get Hobbs if available, then prioritise Ruka, Kang, Rane and Oron from Squin/Shek territory.
2. **Stabilise income and training:** mine copper, buy food/medkits/backpacks, train athletics and toughness before deep travel.
3. **Mongrel run:** collect Beep, Infinite Wingwang, Crumblejon and Shryke if they spawn and you can survive the Fog Islands.
4. **Swamp run:** collect Miu, Green, Doctor Chung, Hamut and Stubs from Mud Town/Shark if available.
5. **Fishing Village run:** collect Green Finger, Soman and possibly Hobbs/Horse.
6. **UC-area run before Anti-Slavers:** collect Jewel, Izumi, Riddly, Red, Ells, Headshot and Ray where possible before hostile UC/slaver relations make travel harder.
7. **Commit to Anti-Slavers / Bo:** only after you have completed sensitive UC-area recruitment and are ready for faction consequences.
8. **Late dangerous rescues:** Agnu, Luquin, Sinklyde and Espher require dangerous rescue operations and should not be early-game targets.
9. **Seto:** treat as a long-term Shek Kingdom progression reward.
10. **Yamdu:** exclude from a Bo/Anti-Slaver run unless you deliberately abandon that moral/faction route, because recruiting him requires turning in Tinfist.
