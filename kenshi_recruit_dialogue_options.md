# Kenshi Recruit Dialogue Options and Cost-Saving Routes

**Scope:** Vanilla Kenshi. This guide focuses on named unique recruits and the recruit routes where public documentation gives enough detail to be useful. It does **not** invent exact dialogue choices where the wiki/community sources only document price, location, or general requirements.

**Important caveat:** The Kenshi Wiki notes that possible spawn locations are not guarantees; some unique recruits may not spawn in a given game unless you import or get lucky with the world state.

## Detailed dialogue notes

### Infinite Wingwang — reduce 100,000 cats to ~5,000

**Use this every time.** Infinite Wingwang initially asks for around 100,000 cats, but the documented route can reduce his cost to around 5,000.

Recommended options:

1. Start his recruitment conversation in Mongrel.
2. When he demands the absurd fee, choose either:
   - **“I'll give you 4,000, and I'm changing your name.”**, or
   - **“What!? I could get 10 Infinite Wingwangs for that!”**
3. When available, choose:
   - **“Um, 5000?”**
4. Pay the reduced price.

**Practical advice:** Do not recruit him until you have 5,000 cats available. The saving is enormous and worth doing correctly.

---

### Cornelius — bargain trap / bug-like behaviour

Cornelius is listed as costing 14,000 cats, with a possible bargain down to 7,000. However, the wiki notes that he still actually costs 14,000 either way.

**Practical advice:** Bring 14,000 cats. Treat the 7,000 dialogue as flavour, not a reliable discount.

---

### Hobbs — free if you humour him

Hobbs can be one of the best early free recruits if he appears in The Hub or a Waystation.

Safe route:

1. Let him talk about his ridiculous legend.
2. Choose options showing interest, such as asking what the legend is about.
3. Continue humouring him.
4. Accept when he asks to join you on the search.

Avoid:

- Insulting him.
- Telling him to leave you alone.

The wiki warns that hostile/dismissive choices can stop you speaking to him again and block normal recruitment for that save.

---

### Beep — free, no money concept

Beep is free in Mongrel. He may follow your squad until you talk to him.

Safe route:

1. Talk to Beep when he follows you.
2. Let him explain he wants to become strong.
3. Ask how much money he wants.
4. He does not understand money.
5. Choose **“Welcome to the team!”**

**Practical advice:** Recruit him during the Mongrel run. Protect him immediately; he starts very weak.

---

### Crumblejon — free if you keep pestering him

Crumblejon is free in Mongrel. The route is not a money negotiation; it is a persistence/comedy route.

Safe route:

1. Talk to him.
2. Keep asking/pestering rather than walking away.
3. He eventually joins out of frustration.

**Practical advice:** Recruit him while already in Mongrel for Beep and Infinite Wingwang.

---

### Miu — free Swamp farmer

Miu is free and is one of the best HQ resource recruits because of high Farming and Labouring.

Safe route from the documented dialogue:

1. Let her rant about the Swamp/spiders.
2. Choose **“Something bothering you?”** or continue listening.
3. Ask what happened.
4. Respond with sympathy or at least do not dismiss her story.
5. Sit/drink with her or let her continue.
6. When she asks to come with you, accept:
   - **“You can come. But no more drink for you.”**, or
   - **“Sure. You could be entertaining to have around at least.”**

Avoid:

- Telling her to go home.
- Dismissing her trauma.
- Ending the conversation before she asks to come with you.

---

### Doctor Chung — free medic if you ask about his problem

Doctor Chung is free and useful as a medic/science recruit.

Documented safe route:

1. Ask about the goons / why they took his chisel.
2. Ask why he does not leave.
3. Choose: **“Well, why don't you come with us? We could use a doctor...”**
4. He joins.

**Practical advice:** Recruit him during the same Swamp trip as Miu, Green, Hamut and Stubs Momuso if they spawn.

---

### Ruka — free Shek recruit, but be respectful

Ruka is free in Shek Kingdom towns. She is usually one of the best early recruits from Squin.

Safe route:

1. Talk to the hornless Shek woman in a Shek bar.
2. Do not mock her horns or shame.
3. Continue respectfully when she explains her past.
4. Answer her battlefield prompt in a non-cowardly way.
5. She joins for free.

**Practical advice:** From a Hub start, check Squin early. Ruka is one of the best low-risk additions if she spawns.

---

### Shryke — free Mongrel recruit

Shryke is free in Mongrel. She is hesitant about leaving the town, especially if your squad has fewer than three members, but documentation says she joins regardless.

Safe route:

1. Talk to her in Mongrel.
2. Encourage her to leave with you.
3. Accept her concerns but keep the recruitment path open.
4. She joins for free.

---

### Sadneil — free if you persist

Sadneil is in the Skeleton Bar in Black Desert City.

Safe route:

1. Talk to Sadneil.
2. Persist / annoy them through the dialogue.
3. They eventually give in and join for free.

**Practical advice:** Good recruit, but Black Desert City is not an ideal first destination from The Hub unless you are prepared for acid/robotics logistics and dangerous travel.

---

### Hamut — free if you oppose slavery

Hamut costs nothing if the interacting character opposes slavery.

Safe route:

1. Talk to him in Shark / Swamp if he spawns.
2. Choose anti-slavery-aligned responses.
3. Do not sound pro-slavery or indifferent to slavery.
4. He can join for free.

**Practical advice:** He fits your Bo/Anti-Slaver strategy well, but exact line-by-line choices are not as clearly documented as Miu or Doctor Chung.

---

### Bo — not a price trick; this is a condition-gated recruitment

Bo is free, but she is difficult because she is not just a normal bar recruit.

Known requirements/conditions:

- She can be recruited if the player is an ally of the Anti-Slavers and has killed or imprisoned one of several qualifying UC / Traders Guild / slaver targets, including Eyegore, Lady Kana, Longen, Emperor Tengu, a Slave Master, or a noble.
- She can also join if the player is already an enemy of the Traders Guild, UC, or Slave Traders, but the player still needs to capture a noble.
- If the player captures Bo, she can be recruited as long as the player is not allied with UC, Holy Nation, or Traders Guild.

Recommended route for your campaign:

1. Do UC-area recruitment and shopping **before** committing to Anti-Slavers.
2. Join/ally with Anti-Slavers when ready.
3. Capture or kill a valid target, ideally one that aligns with your Anti-Slaver route.
4. Return to Bo in Black Scratch and recruit her.

**Practical advice:** Do not rush Bo as your first objective. She is a mid-game recruitment goal unless you are intentionally doing a high-risk Anti-Slaver rush.

---

### Seto — progression-gated Shek recruit

Seto is free once awarded by Esata, but she requires major Shek Kingdom progression.

Known requirements:

- Over 35 relations with Shek Kingdom and killed/imprisoned either Bugmaster or Holy Lord Phoenix; or
- 80 relations with Shek Kingdom.

Important lockout:

- If allied to Holy Nation, Slave Traders, Traders Guild or United Cities, Esata's dialogue to award Seto can be locked off.

**Practical advice:** Seto is a late-game Team 1 recruit, not an early route recruit.

## Practical route implications for your current plan

From **The Hub → Squin → Mongrel → Swamp → UC/Anti-Slaver route**, the dialogue-sensitive order I would use is:

1. **The Hub / Waystation:** check for **Hobbs**. If found, humour him and recruit for free.
2. **Squin:** check for **Ruka** first because she is free and strong enough for early game.
3. **Squin:** pay for **Kang / Rane / Oron / Horse** only when you have the cats; no reliable discount route found.
4. **Mongrel:** recruit **Beep**, **Crumblejon**, **Shryke**, and **Infinite Wingwang**. For Wingwang, use the 5,000-cat bargain route.
5. **Swamp:** recruit **Miu** and **Doctor Chung** for free using their conversation routes; recruit **Green**, **Hamut**, **Stubs Momuso** if available/needed.
6. **UC area before Anti-Slavers:** collect low-cost UC recruits such as **Red**, **Ells**, **Ray**, **Izumi**, **Jewel**, **Headshot** before relations get complicated.
7. **Anti-Slavers / Bo:** only then commit to the Anti-Slaver route and satisfy Bo’s condition.
